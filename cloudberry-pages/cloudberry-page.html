Cloudberry Meadows

About the Project

A soothing website redesign for a small farm. I took the fluffy and dreamy aspects of the brand and translated it into a modern design. It was my first attempt at using UI/UX principles in the creation of this site. I made sure to keep the colours within accessible contrast ranges, and made sure to utilize white space to keep the design clean. I explored differnt typographic styles and tried to maintain consistent theming throughout.

This started out as a wireframe which can be see here: Cloudberry Desktop Wireframe

From this, I got thoroughly learn figma. Utilizing components, grids, columns, prototyping, and plugins. Focusing on a light colour palette to contrast the grit of farming.

Who said farming can’t be dreamy?
